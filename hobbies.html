My Hobbies

First of all, I love solving logic puzzles. Starting from a simple sudoku, going to the harder stuff like the puzzle you can see on the left.

This one on the left was my favorite of last year's end-of-year puzzles of the Belgian Army, known as the hardest bunch of logic puzzles you can find in Belgium.

You can solve these competitively. A team featuring me and two other enthousiasts managed to get second place this year.

In the beginning of the pandemic I bought the boardgame 'Pandemic'. Since then I really got into boardgames.

I love all kinds of boardgames. Ranging from lighter games to play with my family, to full-blown brain burners. The game that is pictured on the right is one of those brain burners and also one of my favorites, Spirit Island.
